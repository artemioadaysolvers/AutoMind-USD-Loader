AutoMind MJCF Viewer BUILD211

Fixes
-----
1. Site-pair equality/connect loops are drawn only as their exact site1-to-site2 residual segments.
   The viewer no longer converts the two collinear connects of a revolute loop into a synthetic joint axis.

2. The "Show Joints" overlay now draws axes only for author-provided tree joints.
   Loop site pairs never appear as cyan artificial axes.

3. Kinematic world matrices are refreshed atomically from post-joint link frames before and after solving.
   This keeps passive/lower links, loop sites, selection pivots and the viewport texture proxy synchronized.

4. Presentation-proxy synchronization now runs before the loop/joint overlay update in every visible frame.

No MJCF XML, joint, loop, coupling, colour or material data is modified by this viewer build.
